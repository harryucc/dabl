dabl Range of integrated products

dabl provides online medical software systems and research solutions for the prevention and management of High Blood Pressure/Hypertension, Stroke, Heart Attack, Heart Disease and a range of other cardiovascular conditions.


dabl also provides a complete package for the implementation, collection and analysis of ABPM and HBPM clinical study data which can be tailored to suit the study protocol.

Improve patient care with dabl expert systems

Accurate diagnosis and ease of management and reporting of 24-hour ABPM (Ambulatory Blood Pressure Measurement). Automatically provides an instantaneous interpreted, one page, blood pressure report for the medical team and patient.

Versions available: dabl ABPM; dabl ABPM Plus; dabl ABPM Pharmacy; and dabl ABPM Health Screening

ABPM and HBPM are now increasingly used both to test anti-hypertensive drug efficacy in clinical trials and also as a cardiac safety biomarker. dabl provides proven centrally hosted systems for ease of implementation and standardised data validation and analysis for multi-centre clinical trials. dabl research

The most advanced cardiovascular - geriatric system available for the assessment and management of the older patient. It comprises the six major quality of life indices to provide an automatic summary for the medical team. dabl geriatric

The dabl National Cardiovascular Shared Care System is a unique initiative by dabl to link all users in a country to a single expert system incorporating all the dabl modules, and sharing a common database. This system will improve patient care with an enhanced shared care service between primary and secondary care, translating to huge productivity and cost saving implications for national health services. dabl sharedsystems

Comprehensive cardiovascular system for hospitals that provides evidenced based diagnosis and management of patients with cardiovascular issues, reducing the number of incidences of stroke and heart attack in the community. dabl cardiovascular

Reduce workloads with dabl efficiency tools

dabl Shared Care

This is an extra facility that is available with each of the dabl products that enables critical patient information to be shared between GPs, Pharmacies, Specialists, and Hosptials

dabl Tracker

Alerts users to any updated information in a patient’s record. It also enables a patient’s medical team to communicate quickly and efficiently.

dabl Diary

Enables specialist units and community care clinics to allocate time slots for direct access by other clinics to book appointments, saving time and costs.

All dabl software systems are hosted online eliminating or reducing the requirement for hardware capital expenditure and in-house IT expertise.
